Blogs

I never considered myself much of a writer (just ask my high school English teachers), but I've always had a profound love for reading. There's something magical about getting lost in a book, stepping into someone else's shoes, and experiencing their world full of triumphs and sorrows.

My appreciation for writing came unexpectedly when invited to be a panelist for a moderated talk. That experience sparked a newfound passion for storytelling and crafting thoughtful, well-researched opinions. As a writer and editor for Ping! — the independent student media body of IIIT-H — I found a rewarding space to explore that passion. Now, as an editor for the MRSD Newsletter at CMU, I continue to promote readership among students and prospective recruiters alike.
